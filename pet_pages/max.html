Max

A grey domestic shorthair cat born in 2011.
Max initially lived his life as an indoor cat.
When we moved to a small farm, he patrolled the field.
Now he is once again an indoor cat,
and doesn't wander any further than the backyard patio.
He becomes a pickier eater as he gets older.
